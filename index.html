SEO & Small Business

Search Engine Optimization is something all small businesses have to consider from the very beginning of their activity. By optimizing their websites for search engines, Texas businesses can acquire a steady stream of qualified leads without having to pay a dime. Nonetheless, SEO services aren’t free of charge. If you want results, you have to be willing to pay the price of a good consultant. The only problem is that the beginning is always hard, especially when you can’t afford to invest too much in your business. If you are in this situation, you may want to use the search engine optimization small business tips and tricks in this article, in order to set a solid foundation to build on.

Keywords

The first thing you should do is to learn everything you can about on-page optimization. There are lots of free guides on the internet, so you’ll be able to do a good job right off the bat, should you take the time to read all these materials carefully. When you do this, you should keep in mind that you have to optimize each of your pages for one main keyword. You probably have several search terms you would like to be found for. However, optimizing only your home page for all these keywords isn’t going to bring you good results. You can do a much better job by assigning each of these terms a dedicated page on your website. This is one of the most powerful search engine optimization small business tips possible. If you implement it the right way, it is going to give you an excellent competitive edge.

Links

Incoming links are the ones that help you build authority and climb your way up in the SERPs. However, you have to make sure you understand what is good and what is bad when it comes to acquiring such links. Google may penalize you if you get caught buying links, so you should be very careful with this practice or avoid it altogether.

The legit way of getting other webmasters to link to your website is to publish the best content you can and help them discover it. Although this practice works very well, creating viral content is extremely difficult. Big companies pay SEO experts huge amounts of money to create such pieces of content. This is something small business owners can’t afford, so you’ll have to unleash your creativity and try to write this kind of content by yourself. It helps to study your target consumers beforehand, as this is the best way to understand what they appreciate most and what they share on their social media profiles and pages.

Images

The images on your website are extremely important. You need to include at least one or two photos on each page. These files need to have names that are relevant for what they contain, as well as for the content of the page they are going to be published on.

Also you need to add an “alt” tag and a “title” tag to each photo. These tags have to be unique and relevant. They are used by search engines for determining what images are about. If your images don’t have alt and title tags, Google and other search engines may not understand they have to rank them for a particular search term. This is another huge search engine optimization small business tip that might bring you a very nice chunk of traffic from search engines. Many people use the image search when they want to find various products.

Loading Speed

The loading speed of your web pages is very important because it is one of the search engine ranking parameters. The faster you site is, the better your chances to rank higher. This means you should do a thorough research when you choose your web hosting provider. If you can, avoid cheap, shared hosting packages, as they don’t offer you too much server power. Virtual servers or even dedicated ones are going to make a big difference in your website’s loading speed, so consider including this expense in your marketing budgets.

As you can see, there are a few things you can do to improve your search engine optimization, even if you can’t afford to hire an expert just yet. By following and implementing the above advice, you’re going to be able to rank well for less competitive keywords. However, if you want to target the monster keywords in your industry, those search terms that can bring you excellent profits, you should consider hiring Marquee Results to help with your SEO needs. You have to be aware that this is a long-term process, so you shouldn’t expect immediate results. If you can wait, you’re going to be rewarded with a generous flow of searchers willing to purchase products or services like yours. Turning these people into paying customers is easy.
