About Us

Ricardo Romanillos

Owner | Marquee Results

Ricardo Romanillos is an entrepreneur and education reformer. Ricardo specializes in search engine optimization and online marketing. His in-depth knowledge of Google is evident in his status as a Google Partner and Local Guide. By driving both organic and paid internet traffic to local small business, Ricardo fulfills his mission to strengthen local small business and improve the economic climate for K-12 education.

Ricardo is deeply invested in the North Texas community. Outside Marquee Results, he works in the field of education supporting the National Alliance for Partners in Equity and Bachman Lake Together.
